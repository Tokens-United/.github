## The problem we solve

Most founders don't have a technology problem. They have a **coordination** problem.

To get a product live, you end up hiring a web agency, a separate dev shop, a freelance designer, a formation agent, and someone to "do the social media" — then spend your week translating between them, chasing handovers, and owning the gaps nobody else will. Every seam between vendors is a place where things break, slow down, or quietly get dropped.

**Tokens United removes the seams.** We are the web agency, the dev shop, the designer, the formation partner, and the team that keeps it all running — under one roof, accountable to one outcome: your business, working. You build the company. We handle everything technical, from day one and for the long run.

```
Discover  →  Form  →  Design  →  Build  →  Launch  →  Scale & Support
```

| Phase | What happens |
|---|---|
| **Discover** | We learn your business, your users, and what "success" actually means — before anyone writes code. |
| **Form** | If you need it: the right jurisdiction, the registered entity, and every digital system to operate. |
| **Design** | Brand and product design in one place, so the thing that ships looks and feels like one coherent company. |
| **Build** | Engineering on a production-grade stack, in the open with you — not behind a curtain until "reveal day." |
| **Launch** | We ship it, watch it, and handle the launch-week issues every real system has. |
| **Scale & Support** | We stay on — new features, performance, security — with the team that already knows your system. |

One team across the whole lifecycle means **you never re-explain your business to a new vendor halfway through.**
