# Support

How to get help, depending on what you need.

## Clients & customers

If you work with Tokens United and need help with a platform we built or operate:

- **Support:** [support@tokensunited.com](mailto:support@tokensunited.com)
- **Website:** [www.tokensunited.com](https://www.tokensunited.com)

## General & new enquiries

Interested in working with us, or have a general question?

- **Email:** [hello@tokensunited.com](mailto:hello@tokensunited.com)
- **Website:** [www.tokensunited.com](https://www.tokensunited.com)

## Reporting a bug or requesting a feature

If you have access to the relevant repository, open an issue using one of our templates:

- **Bug report** — something isn't working as expected.
- **Feature request** — an idea or improvement.

Please search existing issues first to avoid duplicates.

## Security issues

**Do not** report security vulnerabilities through public issues. Follow our [Security Policy](./SECURITY.md) and email [security@tokensunited.com](mailto:security@tokensunited.com) privately.

---

We aim to respond promptly. Thank you for reaching out.
